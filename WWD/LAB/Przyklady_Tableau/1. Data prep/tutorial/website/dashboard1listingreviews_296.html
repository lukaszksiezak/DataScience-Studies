Dashboard 1: Listing Reviews
Now that we've joined the data, we can make a selection on our listings map and visualize how many reviews those listings received.

1 Create dashboard
• Bring in Listings at Lat Long worksheet at the top of the dashboard and Total Reviews worksheet below it
• Click the dropdown at the top right of the Color Legend (appears once you mouse over it) and select Floating > move to an empty area on the map
• Select the funnel icon at the top gray border of to use the Listings at Lat Long worksheet as a filter
