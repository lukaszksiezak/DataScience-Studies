Data Interpreter
Sometimes, the format of the data in your Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets makes it difficult to analyze in Tableau. For example, your data might include sub-tables, hierarchical headers, extraneous headers and footers, or blank rows and columns. The Data Interpreter draws out sub-tables and removes some of that extraneous information to help prepare the data source for analysis.

Connect to the Austin AirBNB Listings.xlsx Excel data set and turn on the Data Interpreter. If you’d like to see how Tableau cleaned up your data, select “Review the results.”
