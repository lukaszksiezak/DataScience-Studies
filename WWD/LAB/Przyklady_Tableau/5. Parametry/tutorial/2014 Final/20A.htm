2.0: Moving avg. – Group exercise (solution)

 

1.      Drag the 18-44 Years Old measure onto the rows shelf and the Year dimension onto the columns shelf.

 

2.      Right-click the AVG(18-44 Years Old) pill > Quick Table Calculation > Moving Average.

 

3.      Right-click the AVG(18-44 Years Old) pill > Edit Table Calculation…

 

4.      Customize…

 

5.      Name the calculated field Moving Avg.: 18-44 Years Old.  Create a new parameter.

 

6.      Name the parameter # of years.  Select integer for the data type.  Click OK.

 

7.      Replace 2 in the calculated field with the # of years parameter.  Click OK twice.

 

8.      Right-click the # of years parameter > Show Parameter Control.

 

9.      Enter selections for the # of years parameter.

 

10.  Drag the 18-44 Years Old measure onto the view, hovering over the y-axis, and release.

 

11.  Finished.
