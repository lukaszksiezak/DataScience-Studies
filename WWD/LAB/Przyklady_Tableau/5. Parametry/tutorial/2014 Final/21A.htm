2.1: Moving avg. – Fitness challenge #1 (solution)

 

1.      Drag the 45-64 Years Old measure onto the rows shelf and the Year dimension onto the columns shelf.

 

2.      Right-click the AVG(45-64 Years Old) pill > Quick Table Calculation > Moving Average.

 

3.      Right-click the AVG(45-64 Years Old) pill > Edit Table Calculation…

 

4.      Customize…

 

5.      Name the calculated field Moving Avg.: 45-64 Years Old.  Replace 2 in the calculated field with the # of years parameter.  Click OK twice.

 

6.      Right-click the # of years parameter > Show Parameter Control.

 

7.      Enter selections for the # of years parameter.

 

8.      Drag the 65-74 Years Old measure onto the view, hovering over the y-axis, and release.

 

9.      Right-click the AVG(65-74 Years Old) pill > Quick Table Calculation > Moving Average.

 

10.  Right-click the AVG(65-74 Years Old) pill > Edit Table Calculation…

 

11.  Customize…

 

12.  Name the calculated field Moving Avg.: 65-74 Years Old.  Replace 2 in the calculated field with the # of years parameter.  Click OK twice.

 

13.  Drag the 75+ Years Old measure onto the view, hovering over the y-axis, and release.

 

14.  Right-click the AVG(75+ Years Old) pill > Quick Table Calculation > Moving Average.

 

15.  Right-click the AVG(75+ Years Old) pill > Edit Table Calculation…

 

16.  Customize…

 

17.  Name the calculated field Moving Avg.: 75+ Years Old.  Replace 2 in the calculated field with the # of years parameter.  Click OK twice.

 

18.  Finished.
