Challenge 1: Who had a higher percentage of tweets sent from an iPhone? The United States, or United
Who had a higher percentage of tweets sent from an iPhone? The United Kingdom, or the United States? Use your new tool "Percent of Total" to determine which country had a higher share of iPhone users on Twitter.
1 Navigate to the "Twitter Search" data source, and drag SUM(Number of Records) to the Rows Shelf
This will give you a bar chart for the total number of tweets.

2 Drag "User Time Zone Country" to the Columns Shelf.

3 Filter to only include the United Kingdom and United States.
This can be done by right clicking "User Time Zone Country" and selecting "Filter..." or by highlighting United States, Ctrl-clicking the United Kingdom, and selecting "Keep Only".

4 Drag "Source" to the Color Pane.

5 Change Number of Records to a "Percent of Total" Table Calculation
Make sure to Right-click SUM(Number of Records) to get to the Table Calculation menu.

6 Let's change the "Compute Using..." to "Source" to ensure we are getting the correct totals.
Now the percent of total will be computed for each source, with the total being the total number of tweets per country.

7 Put on some labels!
