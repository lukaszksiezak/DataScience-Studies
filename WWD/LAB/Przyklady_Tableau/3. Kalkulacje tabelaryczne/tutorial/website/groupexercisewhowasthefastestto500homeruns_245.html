Group Exercise: Who was the fastest to 500 home runs?
Who was the fastest batter to 500 home runs?

Using the "Batting Data" dataset, create a visualization similar to the line chart below. This shows the Top 20 batters (based on Home Runs) and their running total of Home Runs over their career.


1 Drag HR (Home Runs) from the Measures Shelf and place it on the Rows Shelf
Make sure HR is aggregated with a sum (e.g. SUM(HR))

2 Drag Time Since First Year from the Dimensions shelf

3 Change the Marks card from Automatic to Line

4 Drag Player ID onto the Color Shelf, and press "Add all members" when a dialog box pops up.

5 Drag Player ID onto the Filters Shelf, and show the Top 20 by Sum of HR
Navigate to the last tab on the top of the Filter dialog, and choose the "By field" radio button. Enter the top 20 by HR, Sum.

6 Change the SUM(HR) pill to a Running Total
Right click the SUM(HR) pill, and navigate to "Quick Table Calculation". Then select "Running Total".
