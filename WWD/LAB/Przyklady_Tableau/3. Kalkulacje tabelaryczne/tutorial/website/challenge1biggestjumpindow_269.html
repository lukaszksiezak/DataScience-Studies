Challenge 1: Biggest jump in Dow
Using the Stock data set: On which day in 2015 did the Dow see it's biggest jump in value from the previous day? What percent did it increase?
1 Set the Columns and rows shelf
1. Bring the Sum of Value onto the Rows Shelf
2. Bring Date to Columns as a Continuous Day (right-click and drag, to bring up date options)

2 Add Filters
1. Bring Date to the filters shelf and select Years - 2015
2. Bring Index to the filters shelf and select Dow

3 Add the Percent Difference quick Table Calculation to the Sum of Value

4 Hover over the largest spike to check the date and value of that jump in the tooltip
Solution: Aug 26th 2015, 3.952%
