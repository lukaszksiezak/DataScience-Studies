Challenge 2: They say "April showers bring May flowers". Is it true?
They say "April showers bring May flowers". Do rain events in April actually represent a higher percent of total weather events than rain events in May during spring? 
1 Set up the View
1. We've already created the groups 'Season' and 'Events' for you in the Austin Weather Data Set
2. Bring Sum(Number of Records) onto the Columns
3. Bring Season onto the Rows
4. Bring Events(groups) onto Rows after Season

2 Keep only the Spring
1. Right click on the spring season
2. Select Keep Only

3 Add months
Bring discrete Months onto rows between Season and Events

4 Apply Percent of Total to the Sum of records

5 Turn on labels to compare
1. Make sure the Calculation is computing using Table Down
2. May Rain events were 25% while April were 11.96%
