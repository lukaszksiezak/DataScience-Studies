Group Exercise: What percent of each demographic defaulted on their credit card?
How do credit card defaults breakdown by some basic demographics like sex or education level? Create a visualization similar to the screenshot below using the "Credit Card Defaults" dataset.

1 Drag ID from the Dimensions Shelf to the Rows Shelf, and change it to a Count
Protip: Right-click dragging ID from the Dimensions Shelf and placing it on the Rows Shelf will give you different aggregation options! Select Count of ID.

2 Drag "Default Payment Next Month" onto the Color Shelf

3 Change CNT(ID) to a Percent of Total Table Calculation
Right-click CNT(ID), and select "Quick Table Calculation". Select the Percent of Total calculation.

4 Pull the Education (group) and Sex pills from the Dimension Shelf, and place them on the Columns and Rows respectively.
Make sure to place the Sex pill before the CNT(ID) pill.

5 Those numbers don't look right?! We need to change how the Percent of Total calculation is computing. Right click the CNT(ID) pill, and select Pane (down).
