Group: DATEPARSE()
Individual: How long ago did something happen?

Individual: How long ago did something happen?
Create a new field "Length of Credit" that calculates how long current borrowers have had lines of credit.

Then visualize how many people have had credit for any length of time using a distribution (histogram) chart.

1 Create a new calculation called "Length of Credit" that is the difference in years from the earliest line of credit and today's date.
Use the field we created in the previous exercise ([Earliest Cr Line - Date]) for the earliest line of credit in the proper Tableau date format.

DATEDIFF('year', [Earliest Cr Line - Date], TODAY())

2 Drag [Length of Credit] from the Measures pane to the Dimensions pane. Then drag [Length of Credit] to the Columns shelf.
Be sure the view size is "Standard" and not "Fit width" to see your new credit bins.

3 Add a distinct count of [Member Id] to the Rows shelf.
Use a handy trick to specify your aggregation right away! Right-click drag [Member Id] to the Rows shelf, and select "CNTD" as the aggregation. Select "Fit Width" to see all your Credit length bins at once.
